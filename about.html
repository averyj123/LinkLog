LINK LOG

Link Log is a website created for adding all links to one link ! You can share one `link` and everyone opening that link will get your selected links. This will be helpfull in the sharing links on socials. You can have your own Website fully personalised in the section `Create Your Own`. You can add your selected links and then share just a single link. Adding Links to the website is easy take !! You have to update the `links.json` file and the rest work will be done by Javascript !! Have Fun !
